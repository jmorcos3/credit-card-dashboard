Referral bonus: Your Bevy already has a referral link worth 20,000 Marriott pts per referral (up to 5x/year = 100K bonus pts). If anyone you know wants a Marriott card, share your link.

Why never transfer Amex MR or Chase UR to Marriott?

Your Amex MR and Chase UR are worth ~2.0¢ each when transferred to airlines. If you transfer them to Marriott instead, they drop to ~0.7¢ each — you'd lose 65% of their value. Example: 100K MR = a $2,000 Delta One flight to Europe via Virgin Atlantic, OR just $700 in Marriott hotel nights. Always earn Marriott points from Marriott card bonuses and Bilt, and save your premium currencies for flights.
