Card Refresh & Launch Rumors

Credit card issuers typically refresh cards every 2-3 years with new benefits and fee increases. If you sign up before a refresh, you get grandfathered into the old fee for your first year while keeping the new benefits. This is the best time to maximize value.

How the Grandfather Strategy Works

When a card raises its annual fee (e.g., CSP from $95 to $150), existing cardholders keep the old fee until their next renewal date. If you apply right before the increase, you lock in the lower fee for a full year while getting all the new perks added in the refresh. That's free upside.
